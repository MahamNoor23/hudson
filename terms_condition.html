Standard delivery times of goods and services:

The typical delivery period for each product is two weeks, commencing from the day the customer placed the order. If there is an emergency or if the customer specifies a hard deadline, we charge a little bit extra to complete that order, the costs may change based on the style of furniture ordered at our studio.

In addition, we check the address the buyer provided when filling out the database form. We contact you to confirm the order in addition to digitally confirming it. Keep in mind that HUDSON FURNISHING does not have any delivery policies for any furniture category outside of Pakistan. Moreover, we don't take any orders from abroad. We also focus on processing numerous orders as part of a single transaction.

Returning or exchanging items:

If there is any damage or the chosen colour or design was not available, Hudson Furniture offers a 100% free return or replacement. Contact our customer support staff if you are dissatisfied or if you receive an order that does not match the order details you provided. We'll get to work on solving your issue right away. After the process is finished and your complaint is submitted, we will attempt to verify the validity of the situation. Prior to responding, we would first investigate the legitimacy of the complaint. These are the crucial conditions of service for which a buyer feels accountable.

We frequently reply to our consumers and swap out the requested product with a different genuine, high-quality product. Our team would no longer be accountable for the return or the replacement of the product with a new one if we didn't find any legitimate points made.

Terms and conditions of return:

Customers would receive a one hundred percent refund in the form of a cheque sent to their address within fifteen days if HUDSON FURNISHING decided to cancel an order for any reason (such as an absence of stock).

Upon order cancellation by customers,HUDSON FURNISHING, refund the remaining amount (as a Cheque to the customer’s address) after deductions (clearly mentioned under “Cancellation of orders”).

Cancellation of orders:

Non -COD:

-Cancelled on the same day – No deduction except the standard payment transferred fees

-Cancelled after one day – 25% of deduction from total amount

COD:

-Cancelled on the same day – No deduction except the standard payment transferred fees

-Cancelled after one day – 25% of deduction of total amount (appear as negative balance)

-Declined to receive the order without any reason – 50% of deduction of total amount (appear as negative balance).
